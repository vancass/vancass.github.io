Earthlife

TechLadies is a community-led initiative for women to connect, learn and advance as programmers.

As a team comprised of six people, we were tasked to work with an NGO from Earthfest Singapore to build a quiz app that we call "Earthlife".

The quiz app is supposed to raise awareness on how earth-friendly you are, and by the end of it, a to-do list is generated for you to achieve a greener life.

We use Vue.js as the framework and Vuex for the state management. I'm particularly proud of the action cards that you can drag around!
